Newsletters

2009 2008 2007 2006 2005 2004 2003 2001 2000 1999 1997 1996 1986

The TRAA Newsletters have evolved over the years.

The first newsletters were banged out manually on a typewriter and then copied about 100 times for distribution.

These initial efforts were simple (no pictures or colour!) but they did the job.

The latest versions are electronic, emailed to members and other TRAA friends. They feature nice formatting, pictures and yes, colour!

The greatest challenge over the years has been getting editions published on time (or at all!). Such is life within a volunteer organization like the TRAA;

You have to focus your energies on where they are most effective and a newsletter falls further down that priority list.
